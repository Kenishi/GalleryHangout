Gallery Hangout

Share images in your hangout quick and easily!

+ -
What is Gallery Hangout?
Gallery Hangout is a Google+ Hangout App that you can use that allows you to drag-and-drop images into the hangout and share them.

These images will be uploaded to Imgur and then shown in the gallery list for quick browsing.

No more manual uploading and copying URLs or dealing with Google Drive permissions just to show your friends some photos you shot today.

Anything else I should know?
Gallery Hangout is an open source project licensed under the Apache License 2.0. It's free and you can download the source code and do what you want with it. Check it out at GitHub.

In addition, the project is still in development and lacks a certain degree of polish. There may still be a number of bugs.
